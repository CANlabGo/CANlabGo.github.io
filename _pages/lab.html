The Cybersecurity, AI, and Wireless Networks Laboratory (CAN Lab) is founded by Dr. Yili Jiang. The CAN lab aims to design and implement novel solutions for securing data, computing, and networks in real-world applications. The ongoing research projects include

- Intrusion Detection in Internet of Vehicles
- Federated Learning and Explainable AI in IoT
- Defending attacks in Machine Learning
- Privacy-preserving Computing in IoT/Healthcare
